Welcome to the 7R Ranger Handbook, a comprehensive guide to techniques, tactics and procedures used within the 7th Ranger Group.

The handbook is split into two parts. Sections 1 through 8 cover the fundamentals **every Infantryman is expected to know**. Sections 9 through 15 cover leadership and the specialist roles, such as aviation, reconnaissance and armour. Reference material — procedures, regulations and frequently asked questions — closes the handbook.
