## Standard loadout

<img class="transparent-fix" src="/wiki/images/Untitled+drawing.png" alt="Standard eight-person squad loadout">

The current standard loadout for eight people consists of:

- 2x AR
- 2x LAT
- 1x Grenadier (optional)
- Medic
- SL

One LAT is considered necessary to defend against armour, unless you have an MAT
in your squad. An AR is important for suppressing enemies and giving
the squad more room to manoeuvre. Medic and SL are necessary for obvious reasons.

## Adjusting

Loadouts should be tailored to your needs. In the briefing the PL should decide on
the special weapons in the platoon and which squads are to supply them. The SLs can
then tailor their individual loadouts to suit that task.

## General concerns

Make sure you comply with the
[procedures and regulations](/handbook/procedures-and-regulations/) when deciding on
the squad loadout. In particular, make use of the high-magnification scope, and make
sure the second-in-command has a 152.
